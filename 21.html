8. Messi Lifts Fifa World Cup 2022


The year rather ended with a high zeal and sporty spirit with the FIFA World Cup 2022 hosted by Qatar.

Lionel Messi-led Argentina beat two-time world champions France 4-2 on penalties in a high-octane FIFA World Cup 2022 finals. While France trailed, France forward, Kylian Mbappe was highly praised for his performance.
