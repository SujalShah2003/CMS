5. Morbi Bridge Collapse


135 people including women and children were killed after a British-era suspension foot-bridge over the Machchu river snapped in Gujarat's Morbi city on October 30.

Personnel from the Indian Army, the Navy, the Air Force, the NDRF, the SDRF, the local fire department as well as the police department launched search and rescue operations which went on for five days.

Nine persons were arrested in connection with the tragic incident
