4. Queen Elizabeth II Dies


The longest-reigning monarch of Britain and the nation's figurehead for seven decades, Queen Elizabeth passed away on September 8 at the age of 96.

She was laid to rest on September 19, after 10 days of official mourning and her coffin had been in view to the public in Westminster Hall since September 14.

President Droupadi Murmu and Foreign Secretary Vinay Kwatra formed the official Indian delegation at the state funeral of Queen Elizabeth II.
