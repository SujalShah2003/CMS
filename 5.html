5. Ram Mandir Bhoomipujan


PM Modi, UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath and senior BJP leaders attended the groundbreaking ceremony of the Ram Temple in Ayodhya on August 5 - exactly a year after the abrogation of Article 370 in Jammu and Kashmir.

The ceremony was held with all Covid protocols in place - barring relaxation of the restrictions on gathering of more than 50 people - as 175 people attended the groundbreaking ceremony amid the pandemic.
